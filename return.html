Return & Refund Policy

We want every gift to be perfect. Here's everything you need to know about returns.

📅
7-Day Return Window
Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery. After 7 days, we are unable to process any return or refund request regardless of the reason.
🎥 Unboxing Video — Mandatory
▶️
Must be recorded before opening
The video must begin before you touch or break any seal on the outer packaging. It must be one continuous, uninterrupted recording.
📦
Must show outer packaging clearly
The outer shipping box, seal, and any labels must be clearly visible before unboxing begins.
🔍
Must show all contents
Every item inside the package must be shown clearly in the video, including any damage or discrepancy.
🚫
No video = No return
Returns will not be accepted under any circumstances without a valid unboxing video. No exceptions.
✅ Eligible Reasons for Return
  • 💔
    Damaged or defective product — item arrived broken, cracked, or not functioning
  • 🔀
    Wrong item delivered — you received a different product than what you ordered
  • 📭
    Missing item from gift set — one or more items listed in the product description were not included
❌ Not Eligible for Return
  • 🙅
    Change of mind — we do not accept returns because you changed your mind or ordered incorrectly
  • 📂
    Used or opened items — products that have been used beyond the initial unboxing or tampered with
  • 🕐
    Requests after 7 days — return requests raised more than 7 days after delivery
  • 📵
    No unboxing video — any return without a valid unboxing video
  • 🍫
    Perishable items — chocolates and other perishables, unless damaged or defective
  • 🎨
    Personalised gifts — custom-made or personalised items cannot be returned
📲 How to Raise a Return Request
1
Record your unboxing video
Make sure it's continuous and shows the full unboxing from sealed outer box to all contents.
2
Contact us on Instagram within 7 days
DM us at @KasuGifts with your Order ID and the unboxing video.
3
We review your request
Our team will review your video and order details and respond within 2 business days.
4
Refund processed
Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. COD orders receive refunds via bank transfer.
💰 Refunds
  • ⏱️
    Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days.
  • 💵
    For COD orders, refunds are issued via bank transfer — you will need to share your bank details with our team.
  • 🚚
    Return shipping for eligible returns will be arranged and covered by Kasu Gifts.

Need Help? Contact Us

Reach out on Instagram within 7 days of delivery with your Order ID and unboxing video.
